Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 2026

SpudFilms is a product video production service for Amazon sellers. This policy explains what information we collect when you visit spudfilms.com and how we use it. We keep it simple because we have nothing to hide.

What we collect

We only collect information you give us directly through our contact form — your name, email address, and anything you choose to tell us about your product or project. We do not collect any other personal information.

How we use it

We use your contact information solely to respond to your inquiry. We will never sell your information, share it with third parties, or add you to any mailing list without your permission.

Contact form

Our contact form is powered by Formspree, a third-party form service. When you submit the form, your message is transmitted through Formspree's servers and delivered to us by email. You can review Formspree's privacy policy at formspree.io.

Cookies and analytics

We may use basic analytics tools to understand how visitors use our site — things like which pages are visited and how long people stay. This data is aggregated and anonymous. We do not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels.

Data retention

We retain your contact information only as long as needed to respond to your inquiry or conduct business with you. You can request that we delete your information at any time by emailing us.

Your rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information we hold about you. To make such a request, contact us directly.

Contact us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please use the contact form on our homepage.
